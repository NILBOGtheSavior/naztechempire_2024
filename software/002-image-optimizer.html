Image Optimizer

#Web_Development

Description

Locally optimize images for your website. Whether you prefer a one-at-a-time approach or need to process images in bulk, this tool ensures seamless optimization for improved website performance.

Locally resize your images to your specifications using this handy desktop application. With this proprietary app, you can easily resize unlimited quantities of images based on your specifications.

Using bulk processing, you can open entire folders of images for your processing convenience. Using the resizing pane, you can select how you want to crop your image, and select a desired aspect ratio.

With the QuikOptimize function, you can even create batches of different images, creating multiple copies of the same image in different sizes.
